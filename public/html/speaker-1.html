About your Workshop Leader

Balaji Venkateshwar

Balaji Venkateshwar is a Cyber Security Researcher with more than 25 years of experience and expertise in Security Analytics based improvised Security Posturing Hacking & Cyber Defense. He has innovated multiple solutions & created de-novo framework to raise the bar of Cyber Security control and with “two Global Patents” granted, 5 filed & “One Trade Secret”.

He is adept at executing smarter decisions in climates of considerable risk with ever bulging threat matrix for ensuring proactive cyber defense posturing Currently, he is on Advance Study on Cyber Security & use of simulator with an internationally acclaimed research organization (Confidential).

In his last assignment, he has worked as Executive Director- Cyber Security for PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC) and worked on India’s Largest Cyber Defense Project as Key Architect & Designer.

Balaji has also worked for State Bank of India, India’s largest Bank, Wipro as Head (Risk Management & Compliance), Bank of America as Vice President (Global Information Security) for Global Delivery Center & CISO (India) and Secure Matrix, a start-up in Cyber Security Research as Chief Executive Officer.
