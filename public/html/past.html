Convergence India 2017

Exhibitions India Group organized the 25th Convergence India 2017 from 8th - 10th Feb 2017 at Pragati Maidan , New Delhi India ,This exhibition provided an excellent opportunity to showcase the latest products, solutions and technologies in the ICT industry. This B-2-B interface served as an ideal platform for experts to share industry insights on ICT verticals including Telecom, Broadcast, Cable TV, Digital media, Satellite, Smart devices, IT, IoT, Cloud Computing and more.

The ICT ecosystem is expanding rapidly, with endless innovation and new applications of technology. From the convergence of broadcast technology with telecom, IT, and digital media platforms to contactless payments and augmented reality to connected cities technology is changing everything around us.
