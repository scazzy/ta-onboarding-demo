Smart Manufacturing: Industry 4.0

Impact on Shop floor

NASSCOM Center of Excellence - IoT in association with Chamber of Marathwada Industries and Agriculture (CMIA) is pleased to announce “Smart Manufacturing: Industry 4.0 - Impact on Shop floor”

The Industry 4.0 Workshop brings together the Manufacturing and Information Technology Industry to explore the growing vistas of adopting greater and newer technology in today’s competitive environment and be globally ready. 3D Printing, IoT, Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, Cloud, Big Data are opening numerous avenues and possibilities for the industry today.

The Summit offers fresh perspective on the opportunities and challenges in manufacturing industry, and provides the ideal environment to discuss best practices and industry trends.

Venue will also have exhibition stalls displaying Plat on Tab, Augmented Reality/Virtual Reality, 3D Printing technologies etc.
